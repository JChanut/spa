Step Activities

- Familiarize yourself with the broad content of human rights and the available resources

- Understand the implications of the first two principles of the Global Compact

- Develop and encourage a rights-aware approach to your business

- Find out what you are already doing

- Identify risks and opportunities and then the priorities for action.

- Develop a human rights strategy for your business.

- Define and embed appropriate management responsibilities.

- Integrate human rights into your company's activities.

- Develop your strategy through a circle of continuous improvement.

- Include human rights in your existing policies

- Develop specific human rights policies where appropriate

- Develop local policies to meet local situations

- Ensure full implementation of your policies and review their outcomes

- Consider the full scope of your business activities and functions

- Establish procedures for identifying your human rights-related risks and opportunities

- Establish control systems for managing human rights in your business

- Learn from sector-wide business initiatives

- Expect the unexpected – how to react when procedures are not enough

- Share understanding of why human rights are important to business communications

- Integrate human rights into your internal communications

- Integrate human rights into your external communications

- Identify target groups in your business to receive human rights training

- Review the different types of training materials available

- Select, organize and evaluate the training program for target groups

- Set relevant performance indicators for measuring human rights impact across the different functions of your business

- Undertake human rights based audits

- Analyze the results of audits and use the results to inform the strategic development of your business

- Decide which human rights impacts are priorities for you to report on

- Consider who your main target audiences are

- Develop an effective reporting format

- Publish this information on its own or as part of a regular business report

- Submit a link/description to the Global Compact website (Global Compact participants)

  • Policies
  • Processes and procedures
